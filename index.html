We are Things and Stuff and Whatnot (TaSaW), a design
and production studio located in New York, NY. We are
specialized in speculative art, furniture, and objects.
We focus on material and work on a variety of metals,
plaster, textiles, wood and concrete, which allow us
to develop and execute complex projects.

Our clients are generally, but not limited to, interior
designers, architects, artists, collectors, galleries and
institutions.
